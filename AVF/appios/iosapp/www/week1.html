4. Please describe any design and functionality intentions you may have for your Demo app components this month.

My original app is for my church. I chose events because I want them to be able to see their events from the app. I chose camera because if they’d like to add photos and take photos from the app. I’d want Notification to let them know about any upcoming events or reminders about anything. I chose Storage because it’s a Check-in app, anyone who attends the church fills out a “I’m here” card. Its not really for attendance but its mostly for contact info so we can send them things as soon as they leave to remind them about coming back and hanging out with us. Storage would hold all of this information. Lastly I chose Globalization so that anything would happen in our time zone and would be done with our currency. I chose twitter, to be able to follow the pastors or attendars tweets. A place where it would be a tweet field specific to our members. And Facebook to view the facebook page. Members would be able to post their prayer requests straight to the page and see others requests so they could instantly pray if they saw one.
